What is Artificial Insemination?

Artificial insemination (AI) is a modern breeding technique where semen is collected from a carefully selected male animal (sire) and manually inserted into the reproductive tract of a female animal (dam) at the optimal time for conception.

This process replaces natural mating and offers several advantages:

  • Allows farmers to use genetics from superior sires without owning or transporting the male animal
  • Enables precise timing of breeding for maximum success rates
  • Reduces the risk of injury to both animals during breeding
  • Provides better control over genetic selection and herd improvement
  • Minimizes disease transmission between animals

The procedure is performed by trained technicians using specialized equipment and follows strict hygiene and timing protocols to ensure the best possible outcomes.
